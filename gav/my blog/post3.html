Detroit's Creatives

Published on October 8, 2024

Within recent years, A growth in creativity has spawned out of the city of Detroit. Detroit’s music scene is coming back strong, blending its rich history with fresh sounds. Famous for being the home of Motown, the city has always been a leader in music, influencing genres like soul, funk, rock, and techno. Today, new artists are emerging and reshaping what Detroit music means. Artists like Babyface ray, Peezy, Scotti B, and many more have all made head bobbing music with a head turning aesthetic to them that makes outsiders want to see what's within the artist. The underground music scene is especially lively right now. Musicians are working together, mixing different styles, and pushing creative limits. The underground scene right now has created several new sounds that has had an impactful influence on other local creatives and getting recognition from big timers in the city looking at what's next. With recording gear becoming more affordable, many artists are making music from home and performing at local pop-up events. Detroit’s music is more than just fun songs, it tells the stories of the city and its people. Musicians share their real-life experiences, making their music easy to connect with. By working together and supporting each other, these artists are bringing new energy to Detroit’s music scene and helping the city be recognized again as an important place for music.
